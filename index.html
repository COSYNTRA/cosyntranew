What industries does COSYNTRA serve?

We work across multiple industries including E-commerce, FinTech, Healthcare, Education, Travel, Real Estate, and more—offering tailored digital solutions that align with each sector’s unique needs.

What technologies does COSYNTRA use for web and mobile development?

Our team works with leading technologies such as React, Angular, Node.js, Laravel, Android Studio, Flutter, and Swift. We ensure responsive, scalable, and high-performing apps across platforms.

Does COSYNTRA provide end-to-end cloud services?

Yes, we offer complete cloud solutions including cloud migration, infrastructure setup, DevOps, cloud security, and managed services across AWS, Azure, and GCP.

How does COSYNTRA ensure cybersecurity for clients?

We offer a full range of cybersecurity services like VAPT, SOC setup, incident response, cloud security audits, and compliance readiness (ISO, GDPR, HIPAA) to protect your digital ecosystem.

Can COSYNTRA develop AI/ML-based solutions for businesses?

Absolutely. Our AI/ML experts build intelligent models for data analysis, automation, recommendation engines, and predictive systems—customized for your business goals.

What is the typical project engagement process at COSYNTRA?

We begin with a discovery call, provide a detailed proposal, finalize the scope through an agreement, and proceed in phases: prototype, development, testing, and final delivery—ensuring transparency and collaboration at every step.
